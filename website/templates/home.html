Our Services

What Do We Offer..?

Phase One: 4 - 7 Yrs

At this phase children will learn computational thinking - Learning to think like a computer, (The art of listening and following step by step instructions). They will aslo learn computer basics like switching on and off, holding the mouse and typing their names including short drag and drop puzzels.

Phase Two: 8 - 11 Yrs

Here, kids will begin to solve a little complex puzzels, creating some games through drag and drop. They will also be introduced to Computer coding where they have to type in code already made for they. Though they may not understand what it may mean, importance of programming is knowing What it does! This way, their journey will start well.

Phase Three: 12 - 17 Yrs

This age group is ready enough to understand concepts. Beginners are introduced to the short puzzels to just but test their comprehension ability. That done, they are introduced to their first language which is Python Languge (Python Is Very Beginner Friendly). Here, they will learn the basics to advanced of the language by building real_world projects for example, Do To List Apps, Rock Paper Scissors Games all the way to Websites and Blogs.

Phase Four: 18 Plus | Adults

What we offer for 18 Years Plus is not different from Phase Three, Content is all the same, only charges are different. Beginners are introduced to the short puzzels to just but test their comprehension ability. That done, they are introduced to their first language which is Python Languge (Python Is Very Beginner Friendly). Here, they will learn the basics to advanced of the language by building real_wolrd projects like eg Do To List Apps, Rock Paper Scissors Games all the way to Websites and Blogs.

Phase Five: Coaching (Individuals | Groups)

This is for people who wish to learn new things and dont know where and how to start. Advice on how to get started and if neccessary, where to get resources We also include those who wish to verture into Freelancing and Dont have any clue.

Phase Six: Organisations | Entities

Companies and Organisations that may need to work with us in building their projects, seek advice and or Training.
